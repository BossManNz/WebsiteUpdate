Pugly is a distinguished middle-ager in dog years. He joined the firm shortly after birth, starting as Head of Crumbs and quickly rising to Chief Morale Officer. He works a few days a week, schedule dependent on whether the loaf of bread he is decides to get out of bed.

Pugly, often just Pug, is our self proclaimed therapy dog. On arrival he may greet you with his pet unicorn, or another treasure he wants to show off.

He loves a pat, missed the lesson on barking so he is hopeless at guarding anything, and he dedicates most of his energy to taxing anyone who is eating.

If you are lucky, he will follow you into your meeting and provide premium white noise snoring from the corner, or warm your feet while you chat to your lawyer.

Not a dog fan? Tell us and Pugly will be kept out of sight.
