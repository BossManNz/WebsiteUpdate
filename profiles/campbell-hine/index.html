Campbell is a Senior Solicitor in our Family Law team. Campbell graduated from the University of Waikato with a Bachelor of Laws in 2021, and was admitted as a barrister and solicitor of the High Court of New Zealand in 2022.

Campbell understands how stressful family law matters can be, and he takes a calm, practical, and clear approach. Clients receive straightforward advice in plain English.

Campbell believes everyone is entitled to high quality legal representation. When litigation is required, he is a strong advocate.

Campbell appears regularly in the Tauranga Family Court and in surrounding registries.
