Conveyancing

Clear guidance for buying, selling, refinancing, and settlement. Select a tab to learn more.

Buying or selling a home is a big moment. Our team gives you clear, step by step guidance from signing to settlement, plus upfront fee expectations and timelines so there are no surprises. We support clients across New Zealand with efficient, people‑first service. Use the tabs to learn more, or contact us for a simple checklist tailored to your deal.

Talk to a Conveyancing Lawyer

Buying Property

We review the agreement before you sign, explain conditions, and protect your position if issues arise. We coordinate with your broker and bank to keep the finance and settlement timeline on track, and we flag any hidden risks early so you can make confident decisions.

What we cover

  • Agreement review, conditions, and clauses that protect you
  • Title, LIM, rates, and building reports
  • Finance, KiwiSaver withdrawal, and First Home Grant timing
  • Final statements, pre‑settlement inspection checklist, and settlement
Talk to a Conveyancing Lawyer

Selling Property

We help you set the agreement up correctly, manage conditions and timeframes, and handle discharge of mortgage and settlement so funds arrive safely. You get clear communication at each milestone, including any issues that could delay settlement and how we will resolve them.

What we cover

  • Agreement terms, special conditions, and disclosure
  • Title checks and easements that may affect the sale
  • Discharge of mortgage, rates and body corporate calculations
  • Keys, possession, and settlement confirmations

Refinancing and Variations

We coordinate with your new lender, prepare the security documents, and settle the switch with your current bank so there is no gap in access to funds.

Common tasks

  • Loan documentation and certifications
  • Discharge and new mortgage registration
  • Rates and insurance confirmations

First Home, KiwiSaver, Grants

We help first home buyers pull the right levers at the right time, so your KiwiSaver withdrawal and First Home Grant are ready before settlement.

  • First Home Grant eligibility and applications

Due Diligence

Due diligence protects you from surprises. We investigate the title, LIM, and any building or compliance issues, and give you clear advice on risk and options.

  • LIM reports, building consents, and outstanding notices
  • Body corporate records and fees where relevant

Settlement Day
